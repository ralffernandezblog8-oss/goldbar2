GOLD BAR

Welcome to Gold Bar Hotel and Restaurant, a one-of-a-kind destination located in Bag-ong Lungsod, Tandag City, Surigao del Sur, Philippines. Gold Bar Hotel and Restaurant stands as a symbol of comfort and warm hospitality, offering guests a relaxing escape inspired by the charm of coastal living. Our oasis-like resort features 17 well-appointed rooms, a tranquil swimming pool, and Gold Bar Café, our signature dining experience. Designed with both indoor and al fresco seating, the café celebrates the flavors of the region through comforting Filipino dishes, thoughtfully prepared using locally sourced ingredients. Here, meals are meant to be savored—slowly, simply, and in good company. Whether you are staying the night or dropping by for a memorable meal, Gold Bar Hotel and Restaurant is crafted to meet your every need. Wake up to gentle coastal breezes, explore nearby natural attractions, or enjoy the peaceful surroundings of Tandag City before retreating to the calm of the hotel. At Gold Bar Café, we embrace a farm-to-table philosophy, sourcing fresh produce from local farms and daily catches from nearby waters. It’s a celebration of nature’s bounty, served in a setting that invites you to slow down, reconnect, and enjoy life’s simple pleasures. Discover rest, flavor, and authenticity—only at Gold Bar Hotel and Gold Bar Café.
